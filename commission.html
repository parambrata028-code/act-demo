Commission & Payouts

We keep profit sharing straightforward. Below is an example and explanation of how payouts are handled.

Default Example

On realized profit the split (example):

RecipientShare (%)Notes
Investor70%Primary beneficiary — receives majority of profit.

Key points

  • Payouts occur only on realized profits confirmed by broker/escrow.
  • All transfers are auditable and transparent in the investor dashboard.
  • Custom splits per deal can be negotiated and encoded in contract.
